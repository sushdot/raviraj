Our Story

From Elampillai's
looms to your door

Elampillai, in Salem district, is one of Tamil Nadu's best-known handloom weaving clusters — generations of weavers here have turned out cotton and silk sarees that travel across India and beyond.

Raviraj Textiles has been part of that trade for years, supplying Silk, Cotton, Wedding, Soft Silk, Fancy and Printed sarees from our base in Elampillai, Salem, to retailers, resellers and customers across India. This website is our next step: bringing that same direct-from-the-loom catalogue to individual customers, and to online resellers who want a dependable dropship partner.
